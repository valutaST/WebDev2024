Caesar salad

  • Time: 20 min
Ingredients Quantity
loaf 8 slices
chicken fillet 300- 350 g
quail eggs 12 pieces
small tomatoes 12 pieces
Iceberg or Peking cabbag 1/2 head

Coking method

Chicken breast for softness is better to beat off first, and then fry until golden brown in a grill pan with any spices that you like. Then croutons are cooked in olive oil with garlic. The croutons and chicken breast are cooling down. After that, you can start decorating the salad. Tear the salad leaves with your hands, pour a little sauce over them, put the chicken breast cut across the fibers on the leaves, halves of cherry tomatoes and add all the ingredients generously with salad dressing. Mix the salad or serve it in layers - choose for yourself. For example, Maestro Carlini preferred to mix all the ingredients.

Dressing:

  • olive oil 5 tablespoons
  • lemon juice 1 tablespoon
  • granular mustard 1-2 teaspoons
  • sour cream 3 tablespoons
  • honey 1/2 teaspoon
  • salt, dry herbs
  • 1 garlic clove

Greek salad

  • Time: 20 min
Ingredients Quantity
lettuce leaves 100 g
large fresh Bulgarian pepper 1
large fresh tomato 1
fresh cucumbers 3
olives 50 g
onions 1
Feta cheese 150 g

Coking method

With your hands, tear the lettuce leaves coarsely. Coarsely chop sweet peppers, cucumbers, tomatoes. Cut the onion into half rings, knead it slightly. Cut feta cheese (or cheese) into cubes ~ 1x1 cm. Add olives to the salad. Season the salad with salt and drizzle with olive oil. Squeeze lemon juice out of the lemon, pour it over the salad and mix lightly. Also, in order to preserve the beautiful look of the salad, you can not mix, but spread the chopped salad ingredients in parts.

Refueling:
- fresh basil (you can replace 1 tsp dried basil);
- 1 tsp. Dried oregano;
- 2 tbsp. L. Lemon juice;
- salt, black pepper powder to taste;
50 to 70 ml. olive oil.

Source: Greek salad

Lagman

  • Time: 1 h
Ingredients Quantity
Beef pulp 600 g
Noodles for lagman 400 g
Potatoes 300 g
Tomatoes 400 g
Onion 300 g
Carrots 150 g
Tomato paste 2 tbsp.l.

Cooking method

Peel the onion, carrot and pepper and cut them into strips. Pour oil into a preheated cauldron with thick walls, after about 2 minutes, put the onion and fry until golden brown. Add carrots and pepper to the onion, cook over medium heat until soft, stirring constantly. Cut the meat into pieces about 2 cm thick and add it to the cauldron to the vegetables. Fry the meat until a crust forms. Add tomatoes to the meat and vegetables and simmer everything for about 5 minutes. Pour in water, add salt, sugar, black pepper, cover the cauldron with a lid, turn down the heat and leave to cook for 15 minutes. Peel the potatoes, cut them into small cubes and add them to the cauldron. When the vegetable is ready, add the bay leaf and finely chopped herbs. Let it brew. Put the noodles in a pot of boiling water and cook for about 10 minutes. When the noodles are ready, put them on plates and add the meat and vegetables.

Source: Lagman
